Our firm

Ariana Mohammadi, PhD & P1

Ariana Mohammadi is a forensic linguist and a licensed legal practitioner. Ariana earned her doctoral degree in Linguistics from the University of Florida and received a Graduate Certificate in Paralegal Studies from the School of Justice and Emergency Services at Durham College, where she was recognized as the highest-ranking graduate in the program. Ariana is a member of the Law Society of Ontario in good standing and is designated as a Notary Public and Commissioner of Oaths in Ontario. She is further a member of the International Association of Forensic Linguists. For information about consulting services in forensic and legal linguistics, please visit LCCCa.ca.
