Our firm

ARMO Legal Services is a sole practitioner paralegal firm registered in the province of Ontario. The firm provides legal advice and representation in criminal summary conviction charges, provincial offences, and small claims court matters. The firm also represents Ontarians before provincial and federal tribunals and offers notarizing and commissioning services. Ariana Mohammadi, PhD & P1

Ariana Mohammadi is a forensic linguist and a licensed legal practitioner. Ariana earned her doctoral degree in Linguistics from the University of Florida and received a Graduate Certificate in Paralegal Studies from the School of Justice and Emergency Services at Durham College, where she was recognized as the highest-ranking graduate in the program. Ariana is a member of the Law Society of Ontario in good standing and is licensed to provide legal services in the Province of Ontario. She is also designated as a Notary Public and Commissioner of Oaths in Ontario. She is further a member of the International Association of Forensic Linguists.
